# Purpose

The Reporting Engine transforms technical investigation data into a structured intelligence dossier.

The report is not simply a summary.

It is the final product delivered to school administrators after the AI has completed its analysis.

Every report should answer one question:

"What exactly happened, how serious is it, who is involved, and what should we do next?"

The report must remain readable by school administrators while still containing sufficient technical depth for platform auditing.

# Definition of Done

The Reporting Engine is complete only when

✓ Every investigation generates a structured report.

✓ Reports distinguish evidence from inference.

✓ Severity, risk, and confidence are clearly explained.

✓ Evidence is fully accessible.

✓ Reports are exportable in multiple formats.

✓ Reports remain immutable.

✓ Technical appendices support auditing.

✓ Reports are printable.

✓ Administrators receive actionable recommendations.

✓ Reports resemble professional intelligence dossiers rather than AI chat responses.

This specification is authoritative for all report generation, rendering, exporting, and lifecycle management within OverWatch.
