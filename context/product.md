# OverWatch

## Overview

OverWatch is an AI-powered cyberbullying intelligence platform built for secondary schools. It helps school administrators identify, investigate, and document harmful TikTok content directed at their students or institution.

Rather than relying on manual monitoring or student reports alone, OverWatch analyzes publicly submitted or discovered TikTok posts using artificial intelligence to detect cyberbullying, school-targeted harassment, defamation, threats, and other harmful online behavior.

The platform produces evidence-backed investigation reports that enable school authorities to take informed disciplinary action while keeping a human administrator in control of every decision.

---

## Mission

Create a safer digital environment for students by helping schools detect and respond to online bullying before it escalates into real-world harm.

---

## Vision

To become the intelligence platform schools use to monitor, investigate, and understand cyberbullying across social media.

---

## Target Users

### School Administrator

The primary user of the system.

Responsible for:

- Reviewing flagged incidents
- Reading AI-generated reports
- Confirming or dismissing cases
- Managing school settings
- Exporting investigation reports

---

## Core Features

### Incident Detection

Analyze TikTok content for harmful behavior directed toward registered schools.

---

### AI Investigation

Generate structured reports containing:

- Risk score
- AI summary
- Evidence collected
- Detected school
- Detected bullying category
- Confidence score
- Recommended action

---

### Evidence Collection

Collect information from available content including:

- Caption
- Audio transcript
- On-screen text (OCR)
- Comments
- Video metadata

---

### Incident Management

Allow administrators to:

- Review incidents
- Mark false positives
- Confirm violations
- Archive reports
- Add investigation notes

---

### Analytics

Provide insight into:

- Total incidents
- High-risk incidents
- Bullying trends
- Frequently targeted schools
- Detection accuracy

---

## AI Responsibilities

The AI is responsible for:

- Detecting cyberbullying
- Identifying targeted schools
- Classifying harmful content
- Calculating risk scores
- Summarizing incidents
- Producing investigation reports

The AI never makes disciplinary decisions. Final decisions always belong to a school administrator.

---

## Design Principles

- Professional before flashy
- Explainable AI instead of black-box decisions
- Evidence before conclusions
- Human review before action
- Fast workflows with minimal clicks
- Enterprise-grade interface suitable for educational institutions

---

## Success Criteria

An administrator should be able to:

1. Receive a flagged TikTok incident.
2. Understand why it was flagged within seconds.
3. Review all supporting evidence.
4. Decide whether disciplinary action is necessary.
5. Export a professional investigation report if required.
